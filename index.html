About CK Movie Club

Our Vision for Film

CK Movie Club is a new community for movie enthusiasts launching in December 2025. We believe film is more than entertainment - it's a powerful art form that connects people across cultures and experiences.

Our mission is to create a space where students can explore, appreciate, and create films together. Whether you're an aspiring filmmaker or simply love watching movies, you'll find your place here.

We're currently in the planning stages, building partnerships and designing our first season of activities. Join our mailing list to stay updated as we prepare for our grand opening!

Filmmaking Workshops
